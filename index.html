How It Works

Up and running in
under 5 minutes

No downloads. No setup. Works on any device. Your team just needs a browser.

01
🤖
AI Builds Your Quiz

Type a topic or upload your training docs. Claude AI writes the questions, DALL-E 3 generates images for each one — all in under 60 seconds.

02
🎡
Spin to Pick

Use the spinning wheel to randomly select from up to 12 quiz packs. Or browse your library and choose manually. Start with one tap.

03
📱
Players Join Instantly

Share a 6-digit room code. Players join from any phone or laptop — no app needed. They add a selfie photo for the podium moment.

04
🏆
Play & Celebrate

Lightning rounds, bonus multipliers, streaks, power-ups, dramatic announcements, seasonal music, and a selfie podium. It's a full game show.

Features

Everything you need
to run great games

Built from the ground up for team engagement — not another boring survey tool.

🤖
AI Quiz Generation

Describe any topic or upload a PDF — Claude AI writes perfect multiple-choice questions instantly. Great for onboarding, training, and compliance.

Powered by Claude
🎨
DALL-E 3 Images

Every question gets a custom AI-generated image that perfectly illustrates the topic. No stock photos. No generic results. Just perfect visuals every time.

⚡
Live Special Events

Lightning rounds, 2x/3x bonus questions, risky questions, and dramatic full-screen announcements that create genuine excitement.

🎡
Spin the Wheel

A canvas-animated spinning wheel that randomly picks from up to 12 quiz packs. Perfect for recurring game nights with surprise variety.

🔥
Streaks & Power-ups

Consecutive correct answers build streak bonuses. Players get one 50/50 power-up per game. Makes every question feel high-stakes.

📊
Answer Breakdown

Live bar charts after every question show exactly how your team answered. See knowledge gaps, spark discussions, and celebrate correct streaks.

🎵
Seasonal Music Themes

5 synced music themes — Modern EDM, Halloween, Christmas, Summer, Party. Switch live and all players hear the change simultaneously.

📷
Selfie Podium

Players snap a selfie when joining. Top 3 appear on the final podium with their photo, animated crown, and gold/silver/bronze rings.

💡
Fun Facts per Question

Add an explanation or fun fact to any question. It shows on both the host screen and every player's phone after the answer is revealed.
